How to play
Each round, a token starts on a random square facing a random direction (N/S/E/W). The voice reads out numbered instructions, e.g. "1. Go forward two squares. 2. Turn left. 3. Skip a square. 4. Repeat instruction one."

Go back N squares — turn to face the opposite way, then walk forward. You end up facing the reversed direction.
Go backwards N squares — moonwalk: move without turning, so you're still facing the same way as before. Same landing square as "back", different facing!
Turn left / right / around — change facing, no movement.
Skip a square — jump forward, passing over one square. "Skip two squares" = land three ahead.
Sidestep left/right — move sideways without changing facing.
Repeat instruction X — do that numbered instruction again, from where you are now.

Once the instructions finish, teams take turns (the order rotates each round). On your turn, tap your square on the grid, then tap one of the arrows that appear around it to set your facing. Tap Reveal Answer once everyone's guessed: square + facing both right = 🌟 Perfect (+2, no life lost); square right, facing wrong = ✅ +1, no life lost; wrong square = ❌ lose a life. If a team hits 0 lives, they're out — but every other team still gets to finish their turn that round. Last team standing wins!

The grid never sends you off the edge — instructions are always generated to keep you on the board.
