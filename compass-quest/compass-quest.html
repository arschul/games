How to play

Each round, a token starts on a random square facing a random direction (N/S/E/W). The voice reads out a numbered list of instructions, e.g. "1. Go forward two squares. 2. Turn left. 3. Skip a square. 4. Repeat instruction one."

Go forward / back = move that many squares in the direction you're facing (back = without turning).
Turn left / right / around = change facing direction, no movement.
Skip a square = jump forward, passing over one square, landing two ahead. "Skip two squares" = land three ahead.
Sidestep left/right = move sideways without changing the direction you're facing.
Repeat instruction X = do that numbered instruction again, from where you are now.

Once the instructions finish, teams decide where they landed. The teacher taps a team's 🎯 Guess button then taps the square on the grid. When everyone has guessed, hit 🔍 Reveal Answer — correct guesses score a point and keep all lives; wrong guesses lose a life. Last team standing wins!
