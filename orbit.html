How to Play

  1. Click an empty cell to place your marble
  2. Press ORBIT — marbles slide one step clockwise
  3. Get 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win!
  4. Arrows show the direction each cell orbits — plan ahead!
